Chill'n Sweets

Chill'n Sweets is a beloved mobile ice cream truck serving the Mauston, WI area with delicious frozen treats all summer long. We bring the joy of ice cream, popsicles, and snowcones directly to you, whether you're at a local park, enjoying a community event, or just out for a stroll on a sunny day. Our truck is more than just a place to grab a quick treat—it's a nostalgic experience that transports our customers back to the carefree days of their childhood summers. We take pride in offering a wide range of frozen goodies that are sure to delight all ages, from creamy cones and decadent sundaes to fruity popsicles and refreshing snowcones. As a seasonal, family-owned business, Chill'n Sweets is committed to creating a fun, friendly atmosphere wherever we go, always with a focus on customer satisfaction. Whether you’re a long-time regular or a first-time visitor, our goal is to make every experience memorable and filled with smiles. We love being a part of the Mauston community, spreading happiness, and helping to create lasting memories, one treat at a time.

Meet Mary

Mary, a proud Mauston local, is the owner of Chill'n Sweets. With a passion for ice cream and bringing joy to her community, she loves creating sweet memories for families and friends. When she’s not serving up treats from her truck, Mary enjoys spending time outdoors and with her family, always looking for new ways to make Chill'n Sweets even sweeter!
