Frozen Treats

Our menu is a paradise for sweet treat lovers, offering a delightful array of frozen delights to cool you down and satisfy your cravings. Indulge in creamy scoops of our rich ice cream, available in a variety of classic and unique flavors, or try one of our refreshing popsicles bursting with fruity goodness. For a nutritious twist, our açaí bowls are topped with fresh fruit, granola, and honey for the perfect balance of flavor and texture. Treat yourself to a nostalgic ice cream sandwich, a decadent ice cream bar, or a fizzy root beer float that blends creamy and bubbly perfection. If you’re in the mood for something light and refreshing, our vibrant shaved ice options come in a rainbow of flavors to brighten your day. Whatever your preference, we have something to delight every palate!
