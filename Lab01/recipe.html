Iced Caramel Macchiato

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. vanilla syrup or to taste
  • 6 oz. milk
  • 1 oz espresso
  • caramel sauce to drizzle
  • ice

Instructions

Add vanilla syrup to the cup first, then milk, ice and espresso on top.
